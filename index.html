Our Story

At Savoria, we believe in the power of food to bring people together. Our story is one of passion, tradition, and a commitment to excellence. Founded in 1985 by Chef Marco Antonelli, Savoria began as a small family trattoria in the heart of Tuscany. What started as a dream to share authentic Italian flavors has evolved into a culinary destination that honors tradition while embracing innovation.
